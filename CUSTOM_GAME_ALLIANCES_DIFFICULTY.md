# Custom Game, Alliances, and Difficulty

## Custom Game War Room

Open the **Mode Hub** with `F2`, then select **Custom Game**.

The War Room supports these local skirmish sizes:

- 2v2
- 4v4
- 6v6
- 8v8

For every slot, choose:

- **Team A** or **Team B**
- **Human**, **Computer**, or **Closed**
- **MoonGoons Authority**, **Lunar Cartel**, **Null Choir**, or **Hollow Fang Clan**

Select a map, scenario, and difficulty before pressing **Launch Custom Battle**.

### Scenarios

- **Standard Skirmish**: destroy all enemy command structures.
- **Resource Rush**: boosted starting resources and richer deposits.
- **King of the Relay**: central Intel cache creates a contested objective.
- **Last Convoy**: extra convoy supply nodes reshape the map economy.
- **Sudden Death**: low resources with larger opening squads.

### Computer players

CPU slots are playable now. Each computer-owned base receives automated attack orders and periodic reinforcements. Difficulty changes its strength, durability, damage, and reinforcement timing.

### Current human-player scope

The War Room can configure all 4, 8, 12, or 16 player slots. In the current local build, the first Team A human is the direct local commander and the remaining configured human slots are reserved for the next synchronized LAN RTS phase. CPU slots are fully used in the custom battle runtime.

## Global difficulty

Press `F4` anywhere to open the **Difficulty Console**.

| Level | Intended experience |
|---|---|
| Easy | More starting support, weaker/slower AI, stronger recon rewards |
| Standard | Balanced default rules |
| Hard | Tougher, faster AI and reduced starting support |
| Nightmare | Strongest/faster AI, reduced player durability/support, heightened patrol pressure |

Difficulty affects Story Operations, Custom Games, Free Roam, and later waves/reinforcements that appear after a mission starts.

## Free Roam alliances

In **Free Roam: The Fracture Belt**, press `F6` to open the **Alliance Console**.

Recruit one automated partner for the current expedition:

- **Deputy Karr**, MoonGoons Mutual Aid
- **Mox Vell**, Cartel Defector Cell
- **Echo N-7**, Null Choir Witnesses
- **Sable Fang**, Hollow Fang Truceband

Issue one order at a time:

- **Follow**: companion stays in formation behind your vessel.
- **Scout**: companion seeks incomplete recon sites and completes them.
- **Guard**: companion maintains a protective sweep and earns alliance reputation.
- **Salvage**: companion periodically returns Credits from nearby wreck fields.

Alliance work earns reputation during the active expedition. This is the first layer of a longer alliance system that can later carry relationships, favors, faction contracts, and allied AI teams into campaign missions.

- `F2`: Mode Hub
- `F3`: PvP map archive
- `F4`: global difficulty
- `F6`: Free Roam alliance console
- `F8`: in-game chat
- `F9`: developer console
